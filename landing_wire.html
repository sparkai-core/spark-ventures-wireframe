[ 10 ] HONEST ANSWERS
The questions worth asking before you sign.
Q: Do I need a tech background? — No. You need to know your industry and be willing to sell. We handle everything under the hood.
Q: What does Spark own? — Launch License: nothing. We take a monthly fee. Venture Partner: equity, in exchange for deeper involvement from our team.
Q: How is this different from a franchise? — A franchise gives you a territory. This gives you a company — software, customers, and equity you can eventually sell.
Q: What happens after 30 days? — You're in market. Monthly support continues. Strategy calls, network access, and a team that cares whether you're growing.
Q: What can I realistically earn? — Our portfolio average is $60K MRR at six months. Some go faster, some take longer. That's the honest number.
